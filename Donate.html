Why should you donate?

Donating to Sequim High School is a straightforward process that can make a significant impact on the students and the community. Interested donors can visit the school's official website, where they will find a dedicated donation page with detailed instructions. Contributions can be made online through secure payment methods, or donors can choose to send checks directly to the school's administrative office. Additionally, the school often organizes fundraising events and campaigns, providing various opportunities for community members to contribute. By donating, individuals can support essential programs, facility improvements, and extracurricular activities, helping to create a thriving and enriched educational environment for all students.

People should consider donating money to Sequim High School, including its athletics department, to help address critical issues such as decaying buildings and lack of funding. These challenges hinder the ability of students to learn and participate in sports in a safe and supportive environment. Donations can provide much-needed resources to renovate outdated facilities, purchase new equipment, and support programs that enrich students' educational and athletic experiences. By contributing, donors play a crucial role in ensuring that Sequim High School continues to offer quality education and extracurricular activities, ultimately helping students reach their full potential.
