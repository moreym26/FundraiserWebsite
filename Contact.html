This is the contact page

If you're interested in making a donation to Sequim High School, there are several ways to get in touch. You can use these resources below to discuss the details of your donation and how it can best support the students and programs. The administration team is always grateful for contributions and can provide information on specific needs or ongoing projects that could benefit from additional funding. Additionally, you might consider visiting the school's official website through the wolf icon on the home page, where they often have a donations page with more details and options for giving. Your generosity can make a significant impact on the educational experience and opportunities available to the students at Sequim High School.
