About us:

Sequim High School is a vibrant educational community dedicated to fostering academic excellence and personal growth. Nestled in the picturesque town of Sequim, Washington, our school offers a diverse array of programs and extracurricular activities designed to engage and inspire students. From advanced placement courses and vocational training to athletics and the arts, Sequim High School provides a well-rounded education that prepares students for college and careers. Our dedicated faculty and staff are committed to creating a supportive and inclusive environment where every student can thrive. Join us at Sequim High School, where we are shaping the leaders of tomorrow.
